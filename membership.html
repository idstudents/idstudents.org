Become a member of ISA

Students and community members are welcome to join as members of the Idaho Student Association. We are a student-run organization advocating for expanded access and improved quality of higher education to enrich the lives of all Idahoans. By becoming a member, you are supporting us in this important work.

Members receive an exclusive quarterly newsletter with updates on the state of higher education in Idaho, and frequent updates during the legislative session on pending bills which will impact higher education.

Whether you are currently attending college in Idaho, are a proud alum, or are friends or family with Idaho students, we welcome your membership in ISA.
