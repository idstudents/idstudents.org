About Us

Our Mission

  • We advocate for the interest of post secondary students in public policy
  • Ensure adequate state funding for Idaho schools
  • Give a voice to Idaho students in their own education
  • Present policy makers across the state with a unified student voice on education issues

The Idaho Student Association is the only state-wide, student-run organization working to support education. In partnership with the officially recognized student governments and affiliate organizations on Idaho campuses, ISA engages students and policy makers in improving our education.

We believe all Idahoans deserve access to a quality education so they can live a rich, full and successful life, regardless of their racial or socioeconomic background. Democracy, health communities, and the knowledge economy of the 21st century demand a well educated populace. ISA is a non-partisan group advocating for that interest.

As a non-profit, ISA has a 5-member Board of Directors, all of whom must be currently enrolled or have been enrolled as a student in an Idaho post-secondary institution in the past 3 years. The Student Executive Director must be a current student.
